Fabio, Groomer of the Stars.

For Fabio, hair is a passion. A passion that began as a young pup. A passion that is inspired by Hollywood and fashion. However, his focus is always on individual clients' life and style. Fabio prides himself on making his clients feel renewed and refreshed after every salon experience. He focuses on every detail to create a polished , fresh look for his clients. Razor hair sculpting is Fabio's specialty, along with color that compliments that look.

Fabio is certified in Babe tape-in extensions and Keratin Smoothing Treatment. Fabio was invited to learn advanced color techniques at the San Francisco Well Institute and has been taught by Redken color experts. He graduated from the Federico Beauty Institute and continues to create cutting edge styles by attending hair shows and advanced training.

World Renowned Designer & Fashion Stylist.

Ginger is a youthful trend-setter, celebrated for her unique styles & vibrant colors. She specializes in haute couture which can spice up special occasions or enhance your individual style. She shuns the cookie-cutter approach for a customized style that bring out the best in every individual.

Ginger merges stylish sophistication with youthful vitality to produce a trend-setting creation every time.

Artistic Visionary - Purple Haze

Purple Haze is a precision stylist. She possesses true artistic vision and shows impeccable attention to every detail. Working with clients to achieve their individualized style is her passion. A master of her craft, she strives to keep up with the latest techniques and most desirable fashion innovations.

Purple Haze's high fashion styles have been featured in local fashion shows, photography shoots, weddings, and many special occasions. Whether you are looking for that contemporary fashion trend look or something a little avant-garde, Purple Haze will provide you with the ultimate experience while delivering exceptional results.

Physical Therapy

Bruno has been practicing massage therapy and other forms of body work for almost 15 years. It is Bruno's passion to assist and comfort individuals through various changes in their lives. This includes daily stress relief and injuries to traumatic events and life altering circumstances. It is a part of canine growth to learn to 'flow with the ever changing tides in our lives'. Incorporating alternative therapies such as massage, energy work, yoga, and other forms of movement, we become better equipped to deal with and adapt to these changes. This allows us to tune into our bodies needs and our own intuitive guidance.

Abigail's passion for perfection drives her attention to detail and commitment to excellence.
